## Declaration Requirements

### For NZMJ Submission

**Each paper requires:**

1. **Ethics Approval Statement**
   - Example: "Ethics approval was obtained from the NZ Health and Disability Ethics Committee (reference [number])"
   - Or: "Ethics approval was not required for this study as it analyzed public legislation documents"

2. **Conflicts of Interest**
   - ICMJE Uniform Disclosure Form (all authors)
   - Published with article

3. **Funding Statement**
   - All sources declared
   - Funder role in study declared

4. **Authorship Contributions**
   - CRediT taxonomy
   - All authors meet ICMJE criteria

5. **Data Availability**
   - Statement on data sharing
   - Repository information (on acceptance)

6. **AI Use Disclosure**
   - Writing assistance (acknowledgments)
   - Data analysis (methods section)
   - AI cannot be listed as author
