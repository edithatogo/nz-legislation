# Hostile reviewer simulation

## Reviewer 1: methods sceptic
Questions:
- Are the corpus boundaries defensible?
- Is the coding schema reproducible?
- Are the outcomes too dependent on word counts?

## Reviewer 2: policy realist
Questions:
- Does this overstate what legislation can explain?
- Are practical system implications grounded?

## Reviewer 3: NZMJ fit
Questions:
- Is this broadly relevant to clinicians and health leaders?
- Is the prose accessible?
- Is the abstract too technical?
