# Track: P1 - Legislative Volatility

**Status:** ⏳ PENDING

**Research Question:** What is the tempo and pattern of legislative change in NZ health law across reform cycles, and does reform frequency outpace implementation capacity?

**Target:** NZMJ manuscript for 2026 election policy relevance

**Differentiation:** This track focuses on measuring legislative change tempo and implementation gaps, distinct from prior "policy whiplash" framing which emphasized political direction shifts.

## Summary

Analyzes legislative instability in New Zealand health law across reform cycles from 2000-2026. Uses corpus linguistics methods on versioned legislation XML to quantify amendment frequency, textual change rates, and reform cycle impacts.
