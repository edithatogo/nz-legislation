## Key NZMJ Requirements (Summary)

### Manuscript Types and Limits

| Type | Word Limit | Abstract | References | Figures/Tables |
|------|------------|----------|------------|----------------|
| **Original Article** | 3,000-4,000 | 300 (structured) | 40-50 | 4-6 total |
| **Review Article** | 4,000-6,000 | 300 (structured) | 60-80 | 6-8 total |
| **Short Communication** | 1,500-2,000 | 150 (unstructured) | 20-25 | 2-3 total |

**Our research tracks will use:** **Original Article** format (3,000-4,000 words)

---

### Abstract Format (Structured)

**Four headings required:**

1. **Aim** (max 50 words)
   - State research question/objective

2. **Methods** (max 100 words)
   - Study design, setting, participants
   - Key interventions/exposures
   - Main outcome measures

3. **Results** (max 100 words)
   - Primary findings with data
   - Include confidence intervals/p-values

4. **Conclusion** (max 50 words)
   - Main interpretation and implications

---

### Reference Style: Vancouver

**In-text:**
- Numbered sequentially in order of appearance
- Superscript: `Previous research.¹`
- Multiple: `¹,³,⁵` or `¹⁻³` for consecutive

**Reference list format:**
```
1. Smith AB, Jones CD, Wilson EF. Title of article. Abbrev Journal Name. Year;Volume(Issue):Page. doi:xxxx
```

**7+ authors:** List first 6 + "et al."

---

### Figure Requirements

- **Format:** TIFF, EPS, PNG, or JPEG
- **Resolution:** 300 dpi minimum
- **Size:** Single column (87mm) or double column (180mm)
- **Max height:** 240 mm
- **Min font:** 8 point
- **Legend:** Maximum 40 words per figure

---

### Table Requirements

- **Format:** Editable text (Word/LaTeX, NOT images)
- **Each table:** Separate page
- **Title:** Above table
- **Footnotes:** Below table using *, †, ‡, §, ¶

---

### Ethics and Reporting

**Ethics approval:**
- Required for human participant research
- Include committee name and approval number
- Example: "Ethics approval was obtained from the New Zealand Health and Disability Ethics Committee (reference 2023/123)"

**Reporting guidelines:**
- **STROBE** - Observational studies (our tracks)
- **PRISMA** - Systematic reviews
- **CARE** - Case reports
- **CONSORT** - Randomized trials

**Checklist:** Complete and upload as supplementary material

---

### Manuscript Structure

1. **Title Page** (separate file)
   - Title (max 150 characters)
   - Running title (max 50 characters)
   - Authors and affiliations
   - Corresponding author details
   - Word counts
   - Conflicts of interest
   - Funding sources

2. **Abstract** (300 words, structured)

3. **Introduction** (400-600 words)

4. **Methods** (800-1,200 words)

5. **Results** (1,000-1,500 words)

6. **Discussion** (1,000-1,500 words)

7. **Acknowledgements**

8. **References**

9. **Tables and Figures**
