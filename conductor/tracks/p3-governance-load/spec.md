# Specification: P3 - Governance Load

## Title
Governance Load and Administrative Burden

## Research Question
Has governance burden increased in New Zealand health legislation?

## Abstract (Draft)
Health system governance requires balancing accountability with administrative efficiency. This study analyzes trends in governance load and administrative burden in NZ health legislation from 2000-2026 using corpus linguistics methods. We measure regulatory complexity, compliance requirements, and administrative provisions to inform policy debate ahead of the 2026 election.

---

## Corpus Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- **Primary legislation:** Health-related Acts 2000-2026
- **Secondary legislation:** Regulations with governance provisions
- **Versions:** All versions in scope period

### Key Provisions
- Reporting requirements
- Compliance obligations
- Administrative processes
- Oversight mechanisms

---

## Analysis Methods

### Primary Metrics
1. **Reporting Requirements:** Count per Act/version
2. **Compliance Provisions:** Mandatory language frequency
3. **Administrative Complexity:** Section/provision counts
4. **Burden Index:** Composite measure

### Coding Schema
- **Requirement type:** Reporting, audit, planning, consultation
- **Burden level:** Low, medium, high (time/resource intensity)
- **Target entity:** Provider, regulator, ministry, other
