# NZMJ Guidelines - Corrections Applied

**Date:** 2026-03-11  
**Source:** Verified from https://www.nzmj.org.nz/contribute

---

## Corrections Made

### 1. Word Count ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** 3,000-4,000 words  
**Correct:** **3,000 words maximum** (for Articles)

| Manuscript Type | Word Limit |
|-----------------|------------|
| Articles | 3,000 |
| Viewpoints | 3,000 |
| Editorials | 1,200 |
| Clinical Correspondence (Case Reports) | 600 |
| Clinical Correspondence (Medical Images) | 200 |
| Letters | 800 |
| Research Letters | 1,000 |

---

### 2. Reference Limit ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** 40-50 references  
**Correct:** **30 references maximum** (for Articles)

| Manuscript Type | Reference Limit |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Articles | 30 |
| Viewpoints | 30 |
| Editorials | 12 |
| Clinical Correspondence (Case Reports) | 10 |
| Clinical Correspondence (Medical Images) | 4 |
| Letters | 10 |
| Research Letters | 12 |

---

### 3. Abstract Format ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** 300 words, headings: Aim, Methods, Results, Conclusion  
**Correct:** **200 words maximum**, headings: **Aims, Methods, Results, Conclusions**

**Four required headings:**
1. Aims
2. Methods
3. Results
4. Conclusions

---

### 4. Author Listing Rule ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** List first 6 authors + "et al." for 7+ authors  
**Correct:** **List ALL authors if ≤4; first 3 + "et al." if >4 authors**

---

### 5. Journal Abbreviations ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** PubMed/MEDLINE abbreviations  
**Correct:** **Index Medicus** abbreviations

---

### 6. Figure/Table Submission ✅ CORRECTED

**Previous (incorrect):** 4-6 total, separate files with specific dimensions  
**Correct:** **Insert in text where first mentioned**, attach high-resolution versions separately

**Accepted formats:** .jpg, .tiff, .png, .eps

---

### 7. Submission System ✅ ADDED

**URL:** https://nzmj.manuscriptmanager.net

**Required accompanying documents:**
1. Cover letter with specific statements
2. Title page (separate document)
3. Post-acceptance forms (Copyright, ICMJE, Media)

---

### 8. Cover Letter Requirements ✅ ADDED

**Must state:**
- "I (We) accept full responsibility for the conduct of the study, had access to the data, and controlled the decision to publish"
- Any potential conflicts of interest
- All sources of funding

---

### 9. Pacific Terminology ✅ ADDED

**Correct usage:**
- "Pacific" - generic term for group of Pacific nations
- "Pacific peoples" - multiple population groups
- Specific island nation names - for small number of communities

**Avoid:**
- "Pasefika" or "Pasifika" for general Pacific population
- "Pacifica" - not acceptable

---

### 10. AI Use Disclosure ✅ ADDED

**Requirements:**
- Must be disclosed in cover letter AND manuscript
- Acknowledgements section for writing assistance
- Methods section for data collection/analysis/figure generation
- AI **cannot** be listed as author

## Key Takeaways for P1-P5 Research Tracks

1. **Word count:** 3,000 words maximum (tighter than initially planned)
2. **References:** 30 maximum (significantly fewer than 40-50 initially planned)
3. **Abstract:** 200 words with Aims/Methods/Results/Conclusions headings
4. **Author listing:** First 3 + et al. if more than 4 authors
5. **Figures/Tables:** Insert in text, attach high-res separately
6. **Submission:** Via https://nzmj.manuscriptmanager.net
7. **Blinded review:** Manuscript must be anonymised (no author names)
