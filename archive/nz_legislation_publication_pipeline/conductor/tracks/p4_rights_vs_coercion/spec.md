# Spec

## Title
Rights versus coercion in mental health law

## Research question
Has rights language displaced coercive language in mental health legislation?

## Corpus scope
New Zealand health legislation and related instruments, versioned where relevant.

## Primary outputs
- NZMJ manuscript
- supplementary methods appendix
- figures and tables
- policy summary

## Risks
- overclaiming from legal text alone
- unstable corpus boundaries
- symbolic versus substantive language ambiguity
